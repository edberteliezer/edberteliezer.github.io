  • HINGE

    Not Feeling Very Attached?

    One of the most physically used & abused parts of a laptop are its hinges; so it is common that they malfunction. Nonstop back & forth movement with half the laptop’s weight resting on this small part cause hinge issues to get worse as time goes on, as loose screws unravel and broken pieces fracture. We can fix most laptop hinge issues at our store.

    Let Us Find Out The Cause Of Your Problems

    Oftentimes it looks like a hinge issue when it is something else entirely. For example, with cheaper HP & Toshiba laptops, the back cover for the screen that the hinge screws into can be broken. The hinge may be fine, but the part that the hinge screws into is broken. These parts are being made of cheaper and poorer quality plastic as manufacturers race to the bottom to produce $300 laptops. With Apple machines, it is common that the back cover that the hinge screws into comes apart entirely. This piece is glued together on Apple laptops, and to add insult to injury, the exhaust fans blow the hot air out directly onto the glue!

    Come In For A Free Estimate

    The only way to find out exactly what your hinge issue would cost to repair would be to let our technicians open your machine. We do offer no obligation free estimates. This means we’ll open it up and see what’s wrong for free. Our technicians will explain what’s causing the issue and present you with solutions, and you can choose from there what you would like to do. With free estimates you have nothing to lose!

  • OVERHEAT

    Worried?

    If you’re worried that your laptop is running hotter than it should, bring it into our shop for a free evaluation and diagnosis. Each laptop runs normally at a slightly different temperature, and the types of activities you perform can also have a big impact on how hot your laptop should be. This can make it difficult for many people to know whether or not they actually have a problem. The fact is, however, that an overheating laptop will not only be uncomfortable to touch, but it will also cause additional problems the longer you let it go.

    Experienced Technicians

    Our experienced technicians can quickly identify why your system is overheating, and let you know exactly how much it will cost to fix. It may be something fairly small like a clogged fan, or something bigger like a bad heatsink, but no matter what it is, our technicians will be able to fix it up so it is running properly for you again.

     

    We can even offer after-market fans and other solutions to help keep your laptop nice and cool. This is a great solution for power users who demand a high level of performance from their systems. We can help ensure your laptop stays within the ideal operating temperature no matter what programs you’re running.

    Free Evaluation

    So, if you’re worried about your Macbook, or other laptop, don’t hesitate to bring it in for a free, no obligation evaluation. We’ll explain what is causing the issue, and then you can choose whether or not you would like to get it fixed. We look forward to seeing you soon.
